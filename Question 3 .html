Accreditation And Registration

Richfield Graduate Institute of Technology (Pty) Ltd is accredited by the Council of Higher Education (CHE),
registered with the Department of Higher Education & Training as a Private Higher Education Institution under the Higher Education Act, 1997,Reistration Certificate No.200/HEO07/008.
Higher Education Programmes are offered at Higher Certificate, Advanced Certificate , Diploma, Degree, Postgraduate, Honours and Masters level in the Faculties of Information and
Communications Technology; Business, Economics and Management Science.
The institution has a proud track record of 33 years in operation with 140 000 students having completed a range of programmes over this period

Library And E-Library

intelligence (AI) are changing the way libraries work. The institution has taken cognizance of the important role of technology in libraries and has enhanced the libraries to cater for advancing technology
and research in all its campuses
Students have the added advantages of accessing e-Library irresspectie of where they are and, empowering them to draw from a wider range of research material globally. Products of digital resources have
been purchased with the aim of to create a modern, accomplished, user-friendly and simple to use electronic library. The Institution has partned with some of the world's greatest academic digital
publishers such as EBSCO, Elsevier ScienceDirect. Group Publishing, Spinger Nature and PressReader to provide free access to range of e-newspapers, e-magazines, e-books, e-cases and
e-journals to its students.The campus library consits not only of prescribed texbooks but also textbooks of many catergories.
The Annual Academic Library Conference is held virtually.
The conference is held to create an opportunity for academic book publishers, academics and libraries to engage on signifiant issues in support of quality library, information servies & librarianship nationally.
